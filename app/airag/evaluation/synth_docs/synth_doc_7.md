# Municipal Resources and Initiatives

## 1. Harbor City

Harbor City assigned its tidal-energy array to the Blue Current Initiative for year-round grid support.

## 2. Pine County

Pine County contributes its community seed bank to the Heritage Seed Network, which coordinates local crop conservation.

## 3. Mesa Town

Mesa Town placed its solar microgrid under the Sunhaven Resilience Plan to serve critical community facilities.

## 4. River Borough

River Borough manages its restored wetlands as the upstream centerpiece of the Living River Partnership.

## 5. Summit Village

Summit Village shares its avalanche sensor network with the PeakWatch Alliance for mountain hazard monitoring.

## 6. Cedar District

Cedar District operates its mobile health clinic as the traveling unit of the ReachCare Circuit.

## 7. Lake Ward

Lake Ward connected its public rainwater capture system to the Rainwise Commons conservation initiative.

## 8. Orchard City

Orchard City's cold-storage cooperative is the consolidation hub for the FreshRoute Cooperative.

## 9. Delta County

Delta County dedicated its floodplain park to the Room for River Accord as controlled overflow space.

## 10. Granite Town

Granite Town supplies its recycled-glass plant to GlassLoop Works, a regional materials-recovery initiative.

## 11. Willow Borough

Willow Borough designated its electric bus depot as the operating base of the NightCharge Transit Project.

## 12. Prairie Village

Prairie Village made its soil-testing laboratory the analytical center for the GroundTruth Farming Service.

## 13. Coral District

Coral District contributes its reef nursery to the ReefStart Collaborative's restoration work.

## 14. Maple Ward

Maple Ward enrolled its public tool library in the Mend and Share Network for neighborhood repair access.

## 15. Dune City

Dune City incorporated its renewable-powered desalination pilot into the ClearDune Water Project.

## 16. Forest County

Forest County linked its wildfire watchtower network to Fireline Early Alert, a countywide detection initiative.

## 17. Bay Town

Bay Town routes records from its fish-market traceability system into the HarborProof Ledger.

## 18. Cliff Village

Cliff Village committed its cable-car transit link to the HighPath Mobility Link for ridge access.

## 19. Meadow Borough

Meadow Borough registered its pollinator corridor with the BeeLine Habitat Compact.

## 20. Stone District

Stone District assigned its building-heat recovery loop to the WarmStone Exchange district-energy scheme.
